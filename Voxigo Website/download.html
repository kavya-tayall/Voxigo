How to Download Voxigo

Follow these steps to download the Voxigo app on your smartphone:

  1. If you have an **iPhone**, click on the **Apple icon** below.
  2. If you have an **Android phone**, click on the **Google Play icon** below.
  3. Once the app store opens, click the **Install** or **Get** button to download the app.
  4. Wait for the download to complete, then open the Voxigo app and start using it!
