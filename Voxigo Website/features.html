Features

Explore the powerful features designed to make communication easier and more intuitive for neurodivergent children.

AAC Communication Grid

Our intuitive AAC grid helps children express themselves with ease. Featuring customizable pictograms and categories, children can quickly find and select the symbols they need.

  • Customizable categories and pictograms
  • Voice output in multiple languages
  • Sentence building capability
  • Favorites section for commonly used phrases
  • Adaptive learning that suggests words based on usage patterns

Fun Activities

Voxigo offers a variety of engaging activities designed to both entertain and support development. These activities help children express themselves in different ways.

  • Digital musical instruments
  • Coloring books with guided art therapy
  • Fidget games for sensory engagement
  • Audio stories and guided meditation
  • Relaxation exercises and breathing games

Emotional Intelligence

Help children identify and express their emotions with our emotional intelligence tools. These features provide valuable learning opportunities for emotional regulation.

  • Visual emotion cards for selection
  • Strategies for coping with difficult emotions
  • Calming exercises based on emotional state
  • Emotion tracking to identify patterns
  • Customizable responses for different emotions

Communication Insights

Gain valuable insights into your child's communication patterns and development. Our analytics dashboard helps you understand how your child is progressing.

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly usage statistics
  • Most frequently used pictograms and phrases
  • Communication pattern analysis
  • Progress tracking over time
  • Downloadable reports for sharing with therapists

Voxibot AI Assistant

Our AI assistant analyzes your child's usage patterns and provides personalized suggestions to improve communication and engagement.

  • Personalized activity recommendations
  • Communication improvement suggestions
  • Emotional pattern insights
  • Adaptive learning that evolves with your child
  • Regular progress updates and milestones

Complete Customization

Tailor the app to your child's specific needs with our extensive customization options. Control every aspect of the experience.

  • Add custom pictograms and audio
  • Create personalized categories
  • Set feature access permissions
  • Customize the visual appearance
  • Set usage time limits and schedules

Neurodivergent-First Design

Unlike many apps that are adapted for neurodivergent users, Voxigo was built from the ground up with neurodivergent children in mind.

  • Developed with input from specialists in neurodiversity
  • Tested extensively with neurodivergent children
  • Sensory-friendly interface with adjustable settings
  • Simplified navigation with consistent design
  • Reduced cognitive load through thoughtful design

Completely Free

We believe that communication is a fundamental right. That's why Voxigo is completely free with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements.

  • No cost for any feature or functionality
  • No advertisements ever
  • No in-app purchases or premium upgrades
  • No data selling or monetization
  • Funded through grants and donations

Privacy-Focused

We take data privacy seriously, especially when it comes to children. All data is stored locally on your device with optional secure cloud backup.

  • End-to-end encryption for all data
  • No data shared with third parties
  • Optional anonymized usage statistics only
  • Full COPPA and GDPR compliance
  • Complete data deletion option available
