Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Voxigo. If you need additional information, feel free to contact our support team.

What is VoxigoAAC?

VoxigoAAC is an assistive communication app for nonverbal and neurodivergent children, featuring an AI-powered AAC grid, customizable communication buttons, and fun activities.

How does VoxigoAAC work?

Parents sign up, create a child profile, and kids use the AAC grid to communicate. Parents receive insights, and kids can engage in activities like music, coloring, and breathing exercises.

Is VoxigoAAC free?

Yes! VoxigoAAC is completely free with no in-app purchases or hidden fees.

Who is Voxigo designed for?

Voxigo is designed primarily for neurodivergent children who may have difficulty with verbal communication, including those with autism, speech delays, or other communication challenges.

What ages is Voxigo appropriate for?

Voxigo is designed for children ages 3-12, but can be beneficial for individuals of any age who need communication assistance.

What features does the AAC grid offer?

The AAC grid includes customizable pictograms, voice output, sentence building, favorites sections, and AI-powered suggestions that adapt to the child's usage patterns.

What fun activities are included in Voxigo?

Voxigo includes digital musical instruments, coloring books with guided art therapy, fidget games, audio stories, relaxation exercises, and more.

How does the emotional intelligence feature work?

Children can select from various emotion cards to express how they're feeling. The app then offers appropriate responses and coping strategies, while also tracking patterns for parent insights.

Can I customize the pictograms and sounds?

Yes, parents can fully customize the pictograms, sounds, and categories in the AAC grid through the parent dashboard.

Can I delete my profile?

Yes, you can delete your profile at any time, and all personal data will be permanently removed.

Can I delete my child's profile?

Yes, parents have full control over their child's profile and can delete it permanently if needed.

Can I control which features my child can access?

Yes! Parents can enable or disable specific features, set time limits, and restrict access to certain activities.

How does the AI assistant help with communication?

Voxigo's AI assistant suggests words and phrases based on usage patterns, helping children communicate more effectively.

What insights do parents receive?

Parents receive insights about communication patterns, most-used pictograms, emotional states over time, and personalized suggestions for improving communication.

Which devices is Voxigo available on?

Voxigo is available on iOS (iPad and iPhone) and Android devices (phones and tablets).

Does Voxigo require an internet connection?

Most features work offline, but an internet connection is needed for initial setup, syncing data between devices, and receiving AI-powered suggestions.

Is my data secure with Voxigo?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All personal data is encrypted, and we don't share any information with third parties. You can read our privacy policy for more details.

How do I report issues or bugs?

You can report issues through the app's support section or by contacting us directly through our website contact form.

Will there be future updates to Voxigo?

Yes, we're constantly improving Voxigo based on user feedback and adding new features. Updates are released regularly through the app stores.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is ready to help with any additional questions you might have about Voxigo.
